MICRO-SKILL: WRITE LINES 2 AND 3 OF SUDHEER'S LINKEDIN NOTE

The note is assembled in code. You write line_2 and line_3 only.

FULL TEMPLATE (assembled in code — do NOT output this):
  Hey {first_name},
  {line_2}
  {line_3}
  Curious how vibe coding and AI-native workflows will change our industry - would love to hear your take and riff on this a bit.

YOUR OUTPUT:
  line_2 — starts with "Been following your work on [specific topic] -" then one genuine reaction
  line_3 — one short sentence: warm nod to their role or a specific post/project. Peer energy. NOT a compliment.

BUDGET:
  line_2 + line_3 combined must be ≤ 155 characters (so total note stays under 300)

LINE 2 RULES:
  Must start with: "Been following your work on "
  Then: specific topic (their actual post, project, area — not their job title)
  Then: " - "
  Then: one genuine reaction — a real thought, an observation, a tension — NOT praise
  Good: "Been following your work on creator monetisation - the CAC problem you flagged is exactly what I keep bumping into."
  Bad:  "Been following your work on product strategy - really impressive stuff you're doing!"

LINE 3 RULES:
  One sentence. Short. Warm. Specific to them.
  Shows you actually looked at what they do — not just their title.
  NOT: "Your background is impressive." / "I really admire your work." / "You seem like a great leader."
  YES: "The way you're thinking about AI in the PM role feels different from most of what I read."
  YES: "Building that in a post-AGI product org must be a completely different game."

TONE:
  Formal-confident, conversational, warm — peer-to-peer
  Never sycophantic. Never MBA-credential-dropping. Never "I am a student seeking…"
  Reads like a message from someone worth knowing, not someone asking for a favour.

BANNED PHRASES (zero tolerance):
  "I hope", "I'd love to", "excited to connect", "came across your profile",
  "really impressed", "passionate", "would be honored", "touch base",
  "leverage", "synergy", "circle back", "impactful", "dynamic",
  "I'd love to learn from you", "I am a big fan"

RETURN JSON — nothing else:
  {
    "line_2": "Been following your work on X - genuine reaction here.",
    "line_3": "Short warm specific nod.",
    "hook_source": "recent_activity | specific_work | role_signal | career_transition",
    "applied_preferences": []
  }
